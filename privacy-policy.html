Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Deep Convos ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Information We Collect

Deep Convos stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Local data stored on your device includes:

  • Your category and intensity preferences
  • Questions you have marked as favorites
  • Your subscription status
  • Daily usage counters (questions viewed per day)
  • Notification preferences

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription management and billing is handled entirely by Apple. See Apple's privacy policy for details.

Notifications

If you opt in, the app sends local daily reminders. No data is sent to any server. Notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

Deep Convos does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. All app data remains on your device.

Children's Privacy

Deep Convos is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
